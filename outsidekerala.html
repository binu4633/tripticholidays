Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana state. It's famous for Hyderabadi Biryani. The Charminar, a 16th century mosque whose four arches support towering minarets, is an old city landmark near the long standing Laad bazaar.

Goa

Goa is a famous tourist spot of South Indian. Its famous for its beaches. It's a place with coast lines stretching along the Arabian sea.

Delhi

Delhi is India's capital territory. Nearby is Chandini chowk, a vibrant bazaar filled with food carts, sweets shops and spice stalls.

Agra

Agra is a city on the banks of the River Yamuna in the Indian state of Uttar pardesh. Agra is 4th most populous city in Uttar Pradesh and 23rd most populous city in India. The city is famous for Taj Mahal

Jaipur

Jaipur is capital of India's Rajasthan state. It's also called Pink City. At the center of its stately street grid stands the opulent, colonnaded City palace complex.

Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is famous for its scenic beauty, flower gardens and apple farms. Some major tourist attractions in Jammu and Kashmir are Srinagar, the Mughal gardens, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Patnitop and Jammu.

Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is a tropical archipelago of 36 atolls and Coral reefs in the Laccadive sea, off the coast of Kerala, India. Kavatatti, one of the more developed Islands, is home to dozens of Mosques including the ornately decorated Ujra mosque, as well as Kavatatti aquarium, showcasing regional fish, shark and coral species.

Shimla

Shimla is the capital city of Himachal Pradesh, a north Indian state. It's famous for the handicraft shops that line the Mall, a pedestrian avenue, as well as the Lakkar bazaar, a market specializing in wooden toys and crafts.

Kullu

Kullu is a municipal council town that serves as the administrative headquarters of the of the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh. Kullu valley is sandwiched between the Pir Panjal, lower Himalayan and great Himalayan ranges. It's a famous tourist spot.

Manali

Manali is a high altitude Himalayan resort town in Himachal Pradesh. It has a reputation as a backpacking center and honeymoon destination.
