munnar

Munnar is a hill station in Kerala. It's in Idukki district. It's world famous for its cool climate. And it's surrounded by rolling hills dotted with tea plantations established in the late 19th century.

alleppy

Alleppey is famous for back waters. It's also famous for house boats. Alappuzha beach is the site of the 19th century Alappuzha light house.

kumarakom

Kumarakam is a village on vembanad lake in the backwaters of kerala. In the lake, Pathiramanal island is a haven for rare migratory birds.

thekkady

Thekkady is located 4 kms away from Kumily, a plantation town in Kerala - Tamil Nadu border. It is home to herds of Elephants and Tigers.

kovalam

Kovalam is a World famous beach in Trivandrum, Kerala. At the southern end of Lighthouse beach is a striped lighthouse with a viewing platform.

trivandrum

Trivandrum is the Capital city of Kerala. It's distinguished by its British colonial architecture and many art galleries.

eranakulam

Ernakulam is one of the famous cities of Kerala State. It's famous for Marine drive, Bolgatty palace and Fort Kochi. It's famous for Info park too.
